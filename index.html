Our Sound

Forged In Noise

We create music that lives at the edge of heavy and intense – dark soundscapes forged from distortion, aggression, and drive. Every track is built to leave a mark.

Metal Reapers don't follow trends – we create our own. Rooted in the industrial metal tradition but pushing it into new and heavier territory, our sound is constantly evolving, constantly getting louder.

From the studio to the stage, everything we do is built around one goal: to make music that hits you like a freight train and stays with you long after the ringing stops.
